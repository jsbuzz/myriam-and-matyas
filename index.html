How to get there?

Train from Budapest to Tata

We recommend going from Budapest Kelenfold station as it is a smaller station with good connection via metro M4.

Here is the timetable for the trains from Budapest Kelenfold to Tata on the 4th of June.

Airport to train station

You can easily take a taxi from the airport, but only use the ones parking right outside the terminal.
On your way out you will be approached by unofficial taxi drivers, never take a ride with them!

You can book a transfer here: bookinbudapest.com/airport-transfer-budapest

By public transport you can buy the tickets from a vending machine outside the terminal and hop on the bus to one of the Metro stations. From there you can go by M3 to Kalvin ter and change to M4 for Kelenfold station. Here is a link for the route.

You will need a separate ticket for the bus and the metro, but once underground you can change trains with no extra charge.
